Who we are

Ethereal Sound is a network and platform geared towards curating the best in melodic electronic music. Since our inception in November of 2018 we have strived to find the perfect balance between classic atmospheric aesthetics and modern experimental vibes. As Ethereal evolved from a standalone label to not only an artist platform but a fully fledged network, we founded our sister tastemaker pages melo. and Tesseract to give a home to ambient music as well as darker, heavier experimental music.





Join us in our journey to continue to spread truly ethereal music to everyone worldwide.
